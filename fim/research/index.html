Pos-graduate Studies

Mission

The Master´s and Doctorate in Sciences and Engineering program constitutes an effort to expand the educational UABC offer in the postgraduate studies area, in order to meet the highly specialized scientists need, engineers and technologists. Science and engineering students capable of participating in the search and new knowledge generation not only in our country in the international community too.

Vision

The Master´s and Doctorate in Sciences and Engineering program vision is: “To be a leader in the high-level human resources training in the knowledge areas of science and engineering in the country with recognition abroad, promoting scientific development and technology in the region and counting on consolidated and recognized academic bodies”.

Objectives

  • Train researchers in a highest academic level, capable of carrying out original research independently, promoting the elevation of the teaching level and research in engineering, the performance of multi and interdisciplinary studies, as well as the expansion of high-level groups capable of train human resources into the science development and technological innovation in the engineering field in the country.
  • Promote high-level basic and applied research in the region through participation in research projects related with the industry that allow teachers and doctors training in science and engineering with a critical, practical and purposeful approach under a mentoring scheme.
  • Organization and implementation of a pos-graduate program in science and engineering that facilitate the transition from master´s studies to doctoral studies.
  • Strengthen existing research networks and facilitate the way for training and initiate new collaborations with educational institutions in the country and abroad.
  • Respond to society needs in solving national problems in which engineering will be the main engine of new projects development.
  • Strengthen the consolidation process of academic bodies in the academic units involved.

Master's Degree Critical Route

First Semester:

Process
  • Tutor assignation
  • Disciplinary field basic subject and research activities
  • Tutoring sessions
  • Semi-annual report preparation
  • Thesis protocol preparation
Product
  • Approved courses
  • Semi-annual reports
  • Thesis protocol proposal

Second Semester:

Process
  • Disciplinary field subjects and research activities
  • Thesis supervisor assignation
  • Thesis committee assignment in the middle of the semester
  • Tutoring sessions
  • Semi-annual report preparation
  • Protocol presentation at the end of the semester
Product
  • Approved courses
  • Semi-annual reports
  • Registered thesis protocol

Third Semester:

Process
  • Complementary subject and research activities
  • Semi-annual report preparation
  • Tutoring sessions
Product
  • Approved courses
  • Semi-annual reports

Fourth Semester:

Process
  • Complementary subject and research activities
  • Tutoring sessions
  • Thesis defense session
Product
  • Graduate student

Doctorate Degree Critical Route

Stage: Pre-doctorate (maximum 3 semesters)

Process
  • SACC recommended subjects and research activities
  • Thesis supervisor assignation
  • Thesis committee assignation
  • Tutoring sessions
  • Thesis protocol presentation
  • Semi-annual report preparation
Product
  • Approved courses
  • Semi-annual reports
  • Registered thesis protocol

Stage: Candidacy evaluation (optional: maximum 2 semesters)

Process
  • Product presentation that proves their academic competence
  • Doctoral degree candidate exam
Product
  • Academic product
  • Doctorate degree student candidate

Stage: Doctoral thesis development and degree obtaining (maximum 4 Semesters)

Process
  • Tutoring sessions
  • Research activities
  • Article elaboration
  • Semi-annual report preparation
  • Thesis defense session
Product
  • Student article as first author
  • Graduate student
  • Semi-annual reports
  • Doctorate degree thesis
