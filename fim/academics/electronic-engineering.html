Electronic Engineering Program

MISSION

To train human resources of excellence in the field of electronic engineering, competitive in the national and international scene, committed to society and its institution, capable of generating and applying knowledge, as well as developing skills for solving problems in the discipline. In order to improve society's life quality using the engineering state art within a context of values and in harmony with the environment.

Engineers that recognize and practice continuous updating in their specialization area, using scientific knowledge, technological, humanistic in a higher position with a multidisciplinary approach, as well as the foundations, techniques, methods of bioengineering and quality methodologies.

According with current regulations to solve problems in the field of health and insurance of quality in industrial processes, contributing to sustainable development, achieving continuous improvement in production, optimizing technological resources for health sector companies.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

VISION

In 2029, the Electronic Engineer educational program is a consolidated program that trains high-quality professionals, generates knowledge and develops technology that is transferred to the productive sector. It is relevant, innovative and recognized by external organizations, which position it as one of the best in Mexico.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

Admission Profile

Applicants to enter the Electronic Engineer educational program must have the following knowledge, skills, attitudes and values:

Knowledge:

  • Algebra and arithmetic
  • Geometry and trigonometry
  • Physics

Skills:

  • Verbal and written communicative skills
  • Reading skills
  • Interpretation of physical phenomena
  • Problem solving
  • Organization

Attitudes:

  • Discipline
  • Creativity
  • Science and technology interest
  • Proactive
  • Perseverance
  • Teamwork

Core Values:

  • Responsibility
  • Honesty
  • Respect

Graduate Profile

Upon completion of this program, engineers will be able to:

  • Identify, formulate and solve complex engineering problems applying the principles of basic science and engineering.
  • Apply, analyze and synthesize engineering design processes that result in projects that meet specified needs.
  • Develop and conduct adequate experimentation; analyze and interpret data and use engineering judgment to draw conclusions.
  • Communicate effectively with different audiences.
  • Recognize their ethical and professional responsibilities in situations relevant to engineering and make informed judgments that consider the impact of engineering solutions in a global, economic, environmental and social context.
  • Recognize the continuing need for additional knowledge and have the ability to locate, evaluate, integrate and apply this knowledge appropriately.
  • Work effectively in teams to set goals, plan tasks, meet deadlines and analyze risks and uncertainties.

Educational objectives

  • Demonstrate in their professional practice the competence to identify, formulate, analyze, design, build, operate or maintain electronic systems that present engineering solutions with a global vision, in the economic, environmental and social contexts.
  • Are committed to deepen or broaden their knowledge through graduate studies, professional development activities or other appropriate actions.
  • Demonstrate competence to communicate effectively with diverse audiences, work in multidisciplinary groups and show leadership qualities.
