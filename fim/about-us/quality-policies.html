Quality Policies

People who work at Mexicali Engineering Faculty (FIM) of the Autonomous University of Baja California, express their commitment to comply with the legal and social requirements applicable to our academic institution. In order to meet and exceed society expectations, seeking excellence through the OBJECTIVES OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE ACADEMIC QUALITY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM achievement, mentioned below:

  • Have an educational offer of international quality.
  • Maintain and improve the staff quality through competent teachers, to ensure a comprehensive training process for FIM students.
  • Raise the quality of comprehensive university education for students.
  • Contribute to the relevant research development through the formation and consolidation of academic bodies and research lines that allow the generation, application and transmission of knowledge. As well as scientific products that increase the extension and connection with the environment through the committed participation of the FIM students. Generate and support projects through strategic alliances with different social actors, getting the society and industry benefit.
  • Improve practices of ethical and quality management, decision-making based on the current regulatory framework, ensuring an adequate work environment, promoting communication, image and identity. Developing talents in human resources, maintaining and improving infrastructure with highly participation and inclusive internal processes.
